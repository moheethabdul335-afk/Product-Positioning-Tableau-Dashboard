# 📊 Product Positioning Dashboard using Tableau

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyze product positioning and market performance. The dashboard provides insights into sales volume, pricing strategies, competitor pricing, promotions, foot traffic, seasonal trends, and consumer demographics to support business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze average sales volume across product categories.
- Compare product prices with competitor prices.
- Evaluate the impact of product positioning and promotions.
- Understand seasonal sales trends.
- Analyze customer demographics and foot traffic.
- Identify key factors influencing product performance.

---

## 📈 Dashboard 1 – Product Performance Overview
- Average Sales Volume vs Product Category (Horizontal Bar Chart)
- Average Sales Volume by Product Category & Product Position (Stacked Bar Chart)
- Average Sales Volume by Product Category & Season (Heat Map)
- Competitor Price vs Product Price (Scatter Plot)
- Consumer Demographics vs Sales Volume (Side-by-Side Bar Chart)

---

## 📊 Dashboard 2 – Market Analysis
- Foot Traffic vs Average Sales Volume (Scatter Plot)
- Product Category vs Price (Box-and-Whisker Plot)
- Promotion Impact on Price & Sales Volume (Packed Bubble Chart)
